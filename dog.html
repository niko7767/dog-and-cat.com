About Dogs

Introduction

Dogs are domesticated mammals, not natural wild animals. They were originally bred from wolves. They have been bred by humans for a long time, and were the first animals ever to be domesticated. There are many types of dogs which are often kept as pets.

Types of Dogs

There are hundreds of different breeds of dogs. Some of the most popular ones include:

  • Labrador Retriever
  • German Shepherd
  • Golden Retriever
  • Bulldog
  • Beagle

Fun Facts

Did you know?

  • Dogs have an incredible sense of smell, which is 10,000 to 100,000 times more sensitive than humans.
  • The Basenji is the only barkless dog breed in the world.
  • Dogs are known for their loyalty and companionship to humans.
