All About Cats

Introduction

Cats are domesticated mammals, often kept as pets. They are one of the most popular pets in the world. Cats have been associated with humans for at least 9,500 years, and are one of the oldest pet species.

Types of Cats

There are many breeds of cats. Some popular breeds include:

  • Persian
  • Maine Coon
  • Siamese
  • Bengal
  • Ragdoll

Fun Facts

Did you know?

  • Cats have flexible bodies and teeth adapted for hunting small animals such as mice and rats.
  • Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees.
  • A group of cats is called a clowder.
